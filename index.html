Who Is DAV.ID? From a young age, music was my escape from hardships and heartbreaks. For a period of time i just listened and learned until I grew into a person, a man that I felt was brave enough to take over the mantle as a rap star. With the talent to back it up.

About Me: I am 25-years-old ad have been working on perfecting the art of being a rapper since I was 16. Through this journey I have tried to learn from greats like; Drake, Jay-Z, Kanye West, Tupac, Nas and Kendrick Lamar. Even though I am making rap music I want it to still be fresh and new, therefore I tend to incoporate bits of reggae to show off my Jamaican ancestry. Through being born and raised in the GTA, music has always been apart of my daily life and a part of the culture here. Where rapping, singing and writing is kind of everyone's past time. It's our second nature.
